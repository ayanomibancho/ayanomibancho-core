About AyanomiBancho

A simple, high-performance osu! private server backend built using Luvit.

Server Features

🏆

Real-Time Leaderboards

PP calculations and ranks computed asynchronously for all game modes, including support for Relax and Autopilot playstyles.

⚡

High Performance

Driven by LuaJIT's compilation and Luvit's event-driven, non-blocking I/O loop. Response times are kept under 5 milliseconds.

💬

IRC Chat Support

Connect with other players using standard IRC clients or chat with FokaBot, our interactive companion bot.

🎮

Spectate & Multiplayer

Fully supports real-time score streaming, spectating, and custom lobby multiplayer matches directly in the game client.

Server Rules

01

No Cheating / Exploits

The use of third-party gameplay modifiers, macros, cheats, or client tampering is strictly prohibited. Keep the leaderboard competitive and fair.

02

Single Account Limit

Each player is allowed to register and play on exactly one account. Creating multiple accounts (multi-accounting) will result in restriction of all profiles.

03

Respect the Community

Harassment, hate speech, extreme toxicity, and distribution of malicious links or content in chat or profiles are not tolerated.

04

No Chat Spam / Ads

Do not spam channels, advertise other private servers, or cause disruption to chat rooms. Keep discussion constructive and friendly.
