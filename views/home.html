How to Connect

01

Install osu! Client

Download the official osu! game client on your PC and make sure it works.

02

Launch with -devserver

Create a shortcut of your osu! client, open its Properties, and add -devserver o.ayanomi.io.vn to the end of the Target field, or run via terminal:

osu!.exe -devserver o.ayanomi.io.vn
03

Enjoy & Play!

Launch osu! using your new connection, register a new account, or log in with your credentials.
